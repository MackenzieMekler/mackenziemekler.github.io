### Past Research

**Type I Diabetes/Adoptive Cell Therapies**

In my first research experience I explored cellular engineering and how this can be used as a treatment to reorient the body to better fight a disease. In the context of this lab, we explored how re-engineering a specific type of immune cell called a regulatory T cell could hold off the onset of Type 1 Diabetes in mice. Not only could this research go on to the clinical setting one day, it is helping advance our understanding of tolerance in the immune system and autoimmune disease as a whole.

**Streamlit**

I have been thoroughly enjoying exploring the streamlit web design framework.
