Who Am I?

Hey everyone! There is a whole page dedicated to this (see my "about me" for some more fun) but I figured I would give a quick intro. My name is Mackenzie (Mack) Mekler and I am a student. I mean this beyond my PhD studies - which I have only just begun - and more in that learning is a huge part of who I am. It is my favorite past time and encompasses the sciences, engineering, technology, language, sports, and so much more. Following a student I am a builder as creating projects is my second favorite thing to do. This website is a reflection of these loves as I taught myself the code required to make it and then built and hosted it myself. My career shows this as well. As a scientific researcher I am paid to learn and to build things that have never been learnt or built before. This is a dream to me and is part of why I think research called so strongly to me.

Beyond the lab I take up many hobbies. I enjoy language learning having studied many before, French, Portuguese (Brazilian), Mandarin, and to the largest extent Spanish. I also am a sports/games fanatic. I play soccer and volleyball most but can be caught playing pretty much any sport. Additionally, some of my favorite activities with friends include board games like codenames, taboo, hand-to-hand wombat, and exploding kittens.
